WOD

WOD stands for Workout Of the Day and is just that â€“ today's workout

The exercises included in a WOD can be any of the basic lifting moments (Squat, Front Squat, Overhead Squat, Shoulder Press, Push Press, Push Jerk, Deadlift, Sumo Deadlift High Pull and Clean), which are complex and involve several muscles working together. The moments are also functional, i.e. they are movements that are natural for humans to do. In addition, we do pull-ups, push-ups, sit-ups, box jumps, etc. The exercises are many and the combination possibilities are endless - hence the name CrossFit!

Endurance

Endurance WOD gives you an enduring tough workout where we don't focus on the technical movements or the heavy lifting. You will definitely be tired!

Basic

Do you feel like this CrossFit thing is completely new to you? Then it might be a good thing to start with our Basic-WOD, which is a copy of today's regular WOD but a little more simplified and more reviews of all stages. Test!
